While I am only a few years into developing my art, the following grounding points keep emerging in the work I have generated and continue to explore. This list will be modified, expanded and contracted as time goes on through the evolution of my artistic craft.

Process

Engaging with the environment deliberately from a commission, travel sketches or sketches from my local area, I observe the scene with curiosity, noticing the elements that accentuate reality.

These elements often dictate the first medium I use. In completing the initial work, I notice more, looking deeper, stimulating further curiosity to experiment with alternative embodiments.

While not every version is a success, I learn about my mediums, developing skills and techniques through each piece and iteration.
