Art Services by abligh

Art for Sale

I sell my work in person, locally and at Arts on Centre.

Commissions

I am always interested in commissions. If you like what you've seen of my work, but don't vibe with the current pieces available, let's chat!

My Experience

I have close to 20 years experience in consulting on complex projects in a variety of disciplines. From these projects, I have a keen understanding of how to work with folks to define their vision of the future and design a scope of work to meet, if not the full vision, then part of it.

The Process

Once we connect, I'll want to know what ideas you have; my creativity is ignited by collaboration with others. Some of what we will discuss in our first conversation:

  • What in my work caught your eye?
  • What kind of art do you enjoy or are you drawn to?
  • Did you have some ideas of what you would like to have in this piece?
  • Do you have a budget?

Based on this initial conversation, we should have a good sense of if working together is a good fit, and we will proceed from there with sketching out a plan, reviewing some of my other work and pricing.
