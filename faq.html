Art FAQ by abligh

What is your Insta handle?

I am not active on Instagram. This is a deliberate choice which is reflective of my philosophy toward the creation and distribution of my art in my local milieu.

Where are photos of your art?

With the tendency for copying of works and the ingesting of art into AI models when distributed online, I have made the conscious decision to not include my work on my website. This choice also follows from the goal of my work being distributed locally, within the context it can be more relevant and appriciated.

What's the deal with the 02025 (02023, or 02024, or 02026, etc.)? Is it a zip code?

The 02025, 02025, etc. is a way to represent the year with an extra leading digit to inspire consideration of the far future. This concept was originally proposed by Stewart Brand, and is practice encouraged by the Long Now Foundation, an organization he co-founded. Long Now orients its work to get people considering different time scales in their lives and work. This practice helps me consider the flow of time and how the activities we engage in today are influenced by the past and influence the future.
